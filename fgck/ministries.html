Our Ministries

Serving the community, growing in faith, and loving God together.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to grow spiritually, discover their gifts, and make a difference in the community. Youth fellowship, mentorship programs, and outreach activities are part of our mission.

About us

The Youth Ministry at Mutonya Church is a vibrant and dynamic community dedicated to nurturing the spiritual, emotional, and social growth of young people. Our ministry is designed to empower youths to live out their faith boldly, serve others selflessly, and develop a strong personal relationship with God. We believe that young people are not just the future of the Church—they are a vital part of the Church today.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of young people who are spiritually grounded, socially responsible, and actively engaged in fulfilling God’s purpose for their lives.

Our Mission

Women's Ministry

Encouraging women to strengthen their faith, build meaningful relationships, and serve in the church and community. Programs include Bible studies, mentoring, and community support initiatives.

Men's Ministry

Helping men grow spiritually, lead with integrity, and serve their families and community. Activities include fellowship gatherings, leadership workshops, and service projects.

Children's Ministry

Nurturing children in biblical teachings and fostering a love for God through Sunday school, holiday programs, and fun educational activities that teach values and faith.

Music Ministry

Leading worship and glorifying God through music and song. Members participate in choirs, bands, and special performances during church services and events.

Outreach Ministry

Reaching out to the community with love, support, and the message of Christ. Activities include charity drives, visiting the needy, and organizing community development programs.
