About FGCK Nazareth Church

A Christ-centered community transforming lives through love, worship, and service.

Who We Are

FGCK Nazareth Church is a vibrant family of believers committed to sharing the gospel, discipling believers, and creating a warm place of worship and growth for all generations.

As part of the Full Gospel Churches of Kenya, we stand on strong biblical foundations and believe in the transforming power of the Holy Spirit. We exist to bring hope, healing, and spiritual transformation to Nazareth, Mutonya, and the surrounding community.

Our Mission

To win souls for Christ, nurture believers, strengthen families, and serve our community through impactful and Spirit-filled ministry.

Our Vision

To be a thriving Christ-centered church that raises believers who influence generations through faith, unity, and love.

Our Core Values

  • Love: Demonstrating Christlike love to all people.
  • Faith: Trusting God and living by His Word.
  • Worship: Honoring God through sincere worship.
  • Service: Using our gifts to serve the church and community.
  • Integrity: Walking in truth, humility, and righteousness.
  • Unity: Growing together as one family in Christ.
